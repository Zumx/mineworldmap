Many of the great mining heritage sites are well served by public transport — a chance to combine industrial history with railway interest.

## Zollverein, Essen, Germany

Tram-stop 'Zollverein' on Essen line 107, direct from Hauptbahnhof.

## Big Pit, Blaenavon, Wales

Pontypool & Blaenavon Railway heritage line connects from mainline.

## Wieliczka, Poland

ZKA suburban train from Kraków Główny.

## Idrija, Slovenia

Direct bus from Ljubljana; or train to Logatec then onward.

## Kiruna InfoMine, Sweden

On the SJ overnight Stockholm–Narvik line.

## Falun, Sweden

Tåg i Bergslagen suburban train direct.

## Rammelsberg, Germany

RB train to Goslar, then short bus.

## Lewarde, France

TGV to Douai, then short taxi to the mine.

## Beamish, England

Bus from Newcastle Central Station.

## Hallstatt, Austria

ÖBB train Salzburg–Hallstatt with cross-lake ferry.
