December 4 is the feast day of St Barbara, patron saint of miners. Mining festivals worldwide combine religious procession, brass-band music and craft traditions.

## Santa Barbara Day, Cerro Rico, Potosí

Bolivian miners' parade with the saint's image.

## Barbarafest, Zollverein, Essen, Germany

December celebration on the UNESCO site.

## Hradec Coal Festival, Czech Republic

Sokolov coal-basin annual celebration.

## Cornish Pasty Festival, Real del Monte, Mexico

Annual Cousin Jack heritage festival.

## Kernewek Lowender, Moonta, South Australia

Australian Cornish-mining festival, every two years.

## Klondike Day, Dawson City, Yukon

Gold-rush heritage celebration in August.

## Burra Cornish Festival, South Australia

Held in Burra's restored Cornish village.

## Falun Folk Music Festival, Sweden

On the UNESCO copper-mine site.

## Lewarde Saint Barbara Mass, France

The largest French miners' service on December 4.

## Día del Minero, Chile

Working-miners' national day in August.
